# 4. Personas

## Primary persona — Patient ("Maria")

- **Context:** Recently diagnosed with a serious condition; standard treatment has limits or side
  effects she wants to avoid.
- **Goals:** Find trials she might qualify for; understand what participation involves; talk to
  someone.
- **Frustrations:** Medical jargon, endless keyword results, not knowing if she's eligible, fear of
  wasting a doctor's time.
- **Tech comfort:** Moderate; primarily on a phone.
- **Needs from StudyFinder:** Plain language, a personalized shortlist, a clear "what happens next."

## Primary persona — Caregiver ("David")

- **Context:** Searching on behalf of a spouse/parent who is too unwell or overwhelmed to research.
- **Goals:** Do the legwork; compile options; bring a shortlist to the care team.
- **Frustrations:** Managing someone else's medical details; comparing studies side by side.
- **Needs from StudyFinder:** Save & compare, shareable shortlists, ability to search "for someone else."

## Secondary persona — Referring clinician ("Dr. Okafor")

- **Context:** Oncologist / specialist with limited time who wants to point patients to relevant trials.
- **Goals:** Quickly surface a few strong candidates during or after a visit.
- **Frustrations:** No time to parse registries; wants trustworthy, filterable results.
- **Needs from StudyFinder:** Fast filtered search, exportable/shareable list, clinically accurate summaries.

## Secondary persona — Study coordinator ("Priya")

- **Context:** Manages recruitment for one or more trials at a site.
- **Goals:** Receive better-qualified inbound interest; reduce screen-fail rate.
- **Frustrations:** Unqualified inquiries; incomplete information from prospects.
- **Needs from StudyFinder:** Structured hand-off of interested, roughly pre-screened prospects.
- **Note:** Coordinator-facing features are **later phase**; MVP focuses on patient/caregiver.

## Accessibility & inclusion personas

- **Low medical literacy:** needs plain language and definitions ([Glossary](14-glossary.md)).
- **Limited English proficiency:** needs multilingual support ([NFRs](07-non-functional-requirements.md)).
- **Assistive-technology users:** needs full screen-reader and keyboard support.
