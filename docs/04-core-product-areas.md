# 4. Core Product Areas

[← Subscription Tiers](03-subscription-tiers.md) · [Home](../README.md)

StudyFinder is organized into ten core product areas. Each has its own page with functionality and
requirements.

| # | Area | Purpose |
|---|------|---------|
| 4.1 | [Study Discovery](areas/study-discovery.md) | Browse and manage studies across sources |
| 4.2 | [Search & Filtering](areas/search-and-filtering.md) | Narrow study results by relevant criteria |
| 4.3 | [Study Cards & Study Details](areas/study-cards-and-details.md) | At-a-glance summaries and full study information |
| 4.4 | [Scouts](areas/scouts.md) | Review and organize high-potential opportunities |
| 4.5 | [Companies, Sponsors & Contacts](areas/companies-sponsors-contacts.md) | Contact intelligence for outreach |
| 4.6 | [Email Sequences](areas/email-sequences.md) | Automate multi-step outreach |
| 4.7 | [Templates](areas/templates.md) | Reusable email content |
| 4.8 | [Tasks & Reports](areas/tasks-and-reports.md) | Track work and summarize activity |
| 4.9 | [Analytics](areas/analytics.md) | Insight into discovery, scouting, and outreach |
| 4.10 | [User Management](areas/user-management.md) | Access and permission control |

Feature availability across these areas is governed by [subscription tier](03-subscription-tiers.md).
