# 3. Subscription Tiers and Feature Access

[← Product Goals](02-product-goals.md) · [Home](../README.md) · Next: [Core Product Areas →](04-core-product-areas.md)

StudyFinder offers four subscription tiers: **Lite**, **Core**, **Growth**, and **Enterprise**.
Feature availability and usage limits vary by plan.

## Feature access matrix

| Feature | Lite (Free) | Core | Growth | Enterprise |
|---------|-------------|------|--------|------------|
| **Users** | 1 user only; member invitations require an upgrade | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Study Intelligence** | 1 therapeutic-area-only scout; 10 matches/week; no weekly report; All Studies and Companies unavailable | Unlimited scouts; no limits; weekly scout reports; All Studies and Companies enabled | Same as Core | Same as Core |
| **Contact Intelligence** | Not available; upgrade required | Full contact access | Full contact access | Full contact access |
| **TrialTrack CRM** | 1 Opportunity board only; Start-Up board, team management, and analytics unavailable | Multiple Opportunity boards and standard analytics; team management and custom analytics require Enterprise | Same as Core | Full access, including team management and standard/custom analytics and reports |
| **AI FQ Filler** | Included | Included | Included | Included |
| **AI Email Outreach** | Not available; upgrade required | Not available; upgrade required | Included | Included |

## 3.1 Lite Plan

The Lite plan is a free, limited-access option for users who want to explore StudyFinder's study
discovery capabilities.

**Includes:**
- One user
- One therapeutic-area-only scout
- Up to 10 study matches per week
- AI FQ Filler
- One Opportunity board in TrialTrack CRM

**Limitations:**
- No weekly scout reports
- No access to All Studies or Companies
- No Contact Intelligence
- No advanced TrialTrack CRM features
- No analytics
- No team management
- No AI Email Outreach

## 3.2 Core Plan

**Price:** $199 per month

The Core plan provides full Study Intelligence and Contact Intelligence, with foundational TrialTrack
CRM capabilities.

**Includes:**
- Unlimited users
- Unlimited scouts
- Weekly scout reports
- Access to All Studies and Companies
- Full Contact Intelligence
- Multiple Opportunity boards
- Standard analytics and reporting
- AI FQ Filler
- TrialTrack CRM base functionality

**Limitations:**
- AI Email Outreach is not included
- Team management is not included
- Custom analytics and reporting are not included

## 3.3 Growth Plan

**Price:** $299 per month

The Growth plan includes everything in Core and adds AI Email Outreach for automated sponsor and
contact engagement.

**Includes:**
- Everything in Core
- AI Email Outreach
- Unlimited users
- Unlimited scouts
- Weekly scout reports
- Full Contact Intelligence
- Standard analytics and reporting
- AI FQ Filler

**Limitations:**
- Team management is not included
- Custom analytics and reporting are not included

## 3.4 Enterprise Plan

**Price:** $999 per month

The Enterprise plan is designed for organizations that require advanced CRM configuration, deeper
reporting, and team-level management.

**Includes:**
- Everything in Growth
- Unlimited users
- Unlimited scouts
- Weekly scout reports
- Full Contact Intelligence
- Enterprise TrialTrack CRM configuration
- Custom analytics and reporting
- Team management and KPI tracking
- AI FQ Filler
- AI Email Outreach

## 3.5 Upgrade Paths

- **Lite → Core:** unlock full study access, company access, and Contact Intelligence.
- **Core → Growth:** access AI Email Outreach.
- **Core / Growth → Enterprise:** team management, custom analytics, custom reporting, and advanced
  CRM configuration.
