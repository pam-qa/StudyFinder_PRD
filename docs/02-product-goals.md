# 2. Product Goals

[← Product Overview](01-product-overview.md) · [Home](../README.md) · Next: [Subscription Tiers →](03-subscription-tiers.md)

StudyFinder aims to:

- Help users discover relevant clinical studies quickly.
- Make study search and filtering accurate, flexible, and easy to use.
- Support efficient organization of promising opportunities through bookmarks and scouts.
- Provide reliable sponsor, company, PI, and provider contact information.
- Enable structured outreach through templates and email sequences.
- Give users clear reporting and analytics on study discovery and outreach activity.
- Ensure consistent, readable information across study cards and study detail pages.
- Provide clear subscription-based access to features and usage limits.
