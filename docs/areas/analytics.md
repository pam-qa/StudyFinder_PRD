# 4.9 Analytics

[← Tasks & Reports](tasks-and-reports.md) · [Home](../../README.md) · Next: [User Management →](user-management.md)

Analytics provides insight into study discovery, scouting activity, contacts, and outreach
performance.

## Requirements

- Users should be able to select date ranges through a clear date picker.
- Analytics should accurately reflect the selected reporting period.
- Metrics should help users understand study pipeline activity and outreach performance.
- Results should be easy to interpret and consistent with underlying study and email sequence data.
- Standard analytics should be available to **Core**, **Growth**, and **Enterprise** users.
- Custom analytics and reporting should be available to **Enterprise** users.

See [subscription tiers](../03-subscription-tiers.md) for access details.
