# 4.5 Companies, Sponsors, and Contacts

[← Scouts](scouts.md) · [Home](../../README.md) · Next: [Email Sequences →](email-sequences.md)

StudyFinder stores company, sponsor, PI, provider, and related contact information connected to study
opportunities.

## Requirements

- Users should be able to search for companies, sponsors, PIs, and providers.
- Contact search should return complete and relevant results.
- Duplicate contacts should be minimized or clearly distinguished.
- Similar PI or provider records should be reviewed carefully when names differ only by middle name or
  minor formatting.
- Sponsor and company information should remain consistent across related study records.
- Contact records should support outreach actions where permitted.
- Contact Intelligence access should be restricted to eligible [subscription tiers](../03-subscription-tiers.md).
