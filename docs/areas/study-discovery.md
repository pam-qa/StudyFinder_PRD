# 4.1 Study Discovery

[← Core Product Areas](../04-core-product-areas.md) · [Home](../index.md) · Next: [Search & Filtering →](search-and-filtering.md)

StudyFinder provides several areas for users to browse and manage studies:

- **For You** — Personalized study recommendations.
- **All Studies** — Complete study directory.
- **Bookmarks** — Saved studies for later review.
- **ClinicalTrials.gov** — Study records sourced from ClinicalTrials.gov.
- **ClinicalPulse** — Additional study intelligence and opportunities.

Users should be able to open a study, review its details, identify relevant organizations or
contacts, and save it for future action.

> Access to **All Studies** and **Companies** is gated by [subscription tier](../03-subscription-tiers.md);
> Lite users have a therapeutic-area-only scout and a weekly match limit.
