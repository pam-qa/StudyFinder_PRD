# 1. Product Overview

[Home](../README.md) · Next: [Product Goals →](02-product-goals.md)

StudyFinder is a clinical research study discovery and outreach platform that helps users find
relevant studies, organize opportunities, manage sponsor and provider contacts, and conduct outreach
through automated email sequences.

The platform brings study data, contact information, scouting workflows, reporting, analytics, and
outreach tools into one place so users can identify opportunities and act on them efficiently.

## What StudyFinder brings together

- **Study data** — study discovery across multiple sources ([Study Discovery](areas/study-discovery.md)).
- **Contact information** — sponsors, companies, PIs, and providers ([Companies, Sponsors & Contacts](areas/companies-sponsors-contacts.md)).
- **Scouting workflows** — surface and organize high-potential opportunities ([Scouts](areas/scouts.md)).
- **Reporting & analytics** — visibility into discovery and outreach activity ([Tasks & Reports](areas/tasks-and-reports.md), [Analytics](areas/analytics.md)).
- **Outreach tools** — templates and automated email sequences ([Email Sequences](areas/email-sequences.md), [Templates](areas/templates.md)).

Access to these capabilities is governed by [subscription tier](03-subscription-tiers.md).
