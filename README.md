# StudyFinder — Product Requirements Document (Wiki)

**StudyFinder** is a clinical research study discovery and outreach platform that helps users find
relevant studies, organize opportunities, manage sponsor and provider contacts, and conduct outreach
through automated email sequences. It brings study data, contact information, scouting workflows,
reporting, analytics, and outreach tools into one place so users can identify opportunities and act
on them efficiently.
